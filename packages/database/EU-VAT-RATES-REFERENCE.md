## All Rates by Country

### France (FR) - TVA
| Rate | Category | Applies To |
|------|----------|------------|
| 20% | Standard | Electronics, Clothing, General services, Alcohol, Tobacco |
| 10% | Reduced | Restaurant meals, Passenger transport, Hotel accommodation, Cultural events |
| 5.5% | Reduced | Food products, Books, Water supply, Energy products, Social housing |
| 2.1% | Super-Reduced | Press publications, Reimbursable medicines, TV license fees |

**Tax Period:** Monthly
**Filing Deadline:** 24 days after period end
**E-invoicing:** Mandatory from 2026-09-01 (Chorus Pro / Factur-X)

### Italy (IT) - IVA
| Rate | Category | Applies To |
|------|----------|------------|

| 10% | Reduced | Food products, Restaurant services, Hotel accommodation, Construction |
| 5% | Reduced | Basic food items, Social housing, Medical devices |
| 4% | Super-Reduced | Basic food products, Newspapers, Books, Agricultural products |

**Tax Period:** Monthly
**Filing Deadline:** 16 days after period end

## Reduced Rate Categories

### Common EU Reduced Rate Categories (Directive 2006/112/EC Annex III)

1. **Foodstuffs** - Most countries: 5-10%
2. **Water supply** - Most countries: 5-10%
3. **Pharmaceutical products** - Most countries: 5-10%
4. **Medical equipment for disabled** - Most countries: 0-10%
5. **Passenger transport** - Most countries: 6-12%
6. **Books** (physical) - Most countries: 5-10%
7. **Newspapers and periodicals** - Most countries: 2.1-10%
8. **Admission to cultural events** - Most countries: 6-10%
9. **Hotel accommodation** - Most countries: 6-13.5%
10. **Restaurant and catering** - Most countries: 9-12%

## Zero-Rated Categories

### Common across all EU countries:
- **Exports** outside the EU
- **Intra-community supplies** (with valid VAT ID)
- **International passenger transport**
- **International goods transport**

### Country-specific zero-rated:
- **IE:** Most food and drink, Children's clothing, Books, Medicines
- **NL:** (Limited zero-rating, mostly standard or reduced)

## Exempt Categories

Common VAT exemptions (Directive 2006/112/EC):
- Healthcare and medical services
- Education and training
- Financial services
- Insurance services
- Postal services (national)
- Cultural services (specific)
- Sports and physical education
- Non-profit organizations (specific activities)

**Note:** Exempt ≠ Zero-rated. Exempt means no VAT charged and no input VAT recovery.

## Special Schemes

### Margin Schemes
- Second-hand goods
- Works of art
- Antiques and collectors' items

### Flat-Rate Schemes
- Small businesses (country-specific thresholds)
- Farmers

## Intra-Community Supplies

### Requirements for Zero-Rating:
1. Valid VAT ID of customer (VIES verification)
2. Goods physically moved to another EU country
3. Evidence of transport
4. Proper documentation (commercial invoice)
5. Recapitulative statement (EC Sales List)

### VIES Validation
All countries require VIES validation for B2B intra-community supplies.

## SAF-T Requirements

**Countries requiring SAF-T:**
- Netherlands (NL) - Annual
- Ireland (IE) - Annual
- Austria (AT) - On demand

## Invoice Numbering Requirements

All countries require:
- Sequential numbering (no gaps allowed)
- Unique per invoice
- No reuse of numbers

Some countries allow:
- Year prefix (e.g., 2025-001)
- Multiple series (with clear separation)
- Custom prefixes (e.g., INV-2025-001)
